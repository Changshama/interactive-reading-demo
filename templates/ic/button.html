Don’t Push the Button



  • Bill Cotter

    • Bill Cotter is a renowned children's book author and illustrator. Cotter’s art is clean and simple with monsters and button posed against plenty of white space, keeping the focus tightly on the action.


  • What is the story about?

    • The only rule in Larry's book is that the reader not push the button, but when no one is looking, it may be irresistible.

  • Reading tips

    • Impulse control can be a challenge for many young children. You can help your kids learn improve their impulsive control by establishing household rules.

    This book is a fun way to present negative consequences of breaking the rule of not pressing the button. Read the book with your kids, and help them interpret situations and guide them toward better choices.
