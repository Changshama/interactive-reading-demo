Train Your Angry Dragon



  • Steve Herman

    • Steve Herman is a best-selling children's books author. He is passionate about teaching children valuable social and emotional life lessons through his cute, fun, and relatable children's books. Steve's books have been used by hundreds of thousands of parents, educators, and mental health professionals to help kids deal with Social-Emotional challenges in a fun and effortless way.


  • What is the story about?

    • Written by Steve Herman, Train Your Angry Dragon is a story of a boy named Anthony and his pet dragon, Diggory. This is a cute children story to teach kids about emotions and anger management. More importantly, the skills from the book are highly practical.

  • Reading tips

    • Prompt your kids to connect with their own experience. Has he or she experienced bully as Diggory did? How did he or she respond?
