    • The ‘Oshiri Tantei’ series were created by Tororu (the pseudonym for the creative team of writer Yōko Tanaka and illustrator Masahide Fukasawa). Oshiri Tantei literally translates as ‘Butt Detective’. This series were among the most popular children's book in Japan.

  • Bonus

    • The butt detective Oshiri Tantei's image is actually based on a rare birth defect, which is Torsonic Polarity Syndrome, or TPS for short.

    This condition causes a person to grow a buttocks where their head should be, making the affected a literal "butt-head". According to Stephen and Martha Thompson, more than eleven people worldwide suffer from this condition and although some may think that they would have faces where their buttocks would normally be, this is not the case. Underneath the buttocks is a fully functional face with a full set of fully functioning organs.

    In South Park Season Five, the episode "How to eat with your butt" depicted two characters with this birth defect.
