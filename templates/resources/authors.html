Award winning authors



  • Mo Willems

    • Mo Willems is one of the best authors who sees the world from children's eyes. In his books, he speaks with a child's voice as rationalizing the decisions. Check out one of his best book series, the pigeon series: Don't let the pigeon drive the bus, Don't let the pigeon stay up late, The pigeon needs a bath, The pigeon has to go to school, The pigeon wants a puppy, etc.

  • Eric Carle

    • Eric Carle is a creator, author, and illustrator of many classic children's books. His stories are simple and refine, logical and harmonious. He likes using simple shapes, bright colors, and a lot of white spaces in his illustations. His picture book The very hungry caterpillar has been translated into more than 66 languages and sold more than 50 million copies. Check out this classic series: The very hungry caterpillar's first summer, The very hungry caterpillar's first spring, The very hungry caterpillar's first winter, The very hungry caterpillar's first fall.

  • Sydney Smith

    • Sydney Smith is a Canadian author and illustrator. Smith's stories are among the most powerful books about empathy and self-acceptance. His painting style is unqiue, lyrical, color-saturatred, and empowering. Check out his award winning books, I talk like a river, Town is by the sea, Small in the city
