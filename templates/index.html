How does CoCoRead work?

For each book, CoCoRead provides a set of pedagogical questions designed for readers to reflect on the content during reading. The AI bot CoCo picks questions for the reader, the reader responds to those questions, and CoCo analyzes the answers and adapts the future question selection to the reader's cognitive and language level. Why does CoCoRead help reading?

CoCoRead leverages conversational reading, combining reading, listening and speaking all together. It is a research proven method of effective language learning pedagogy. First, by answering the questions in the conversations, readers have a better reading comprehension. Second, by communicating with the AI bot, readers learn how to use the vocabularies and phrasal verbs from reading materials. Therefore, CoCoRead promotes both linguistic compentence and communicative compentence of the readers.
