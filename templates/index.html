How does CoCoRead work?

For each book, CoCoRead provides a set of pedagogical questions designed for children to better understand the content. The AI bot CoCo picks the right questions for the reader, the reader answers those questions, and CoCo analyzes the answers and adapts future question selection to the reader's level.

Why does CoCoRead help reading?

Conversational reading is a research proven method of effective learning. It results in higher engagement and better comprehension.
