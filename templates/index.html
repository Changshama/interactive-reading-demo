How does CoCoRead work?

For each book, CoCoRead provides a set of pedagogical questions designed for children to reflect on the content. The AI bot CoCo picks questions for the reader, the reader responds to those questions, and CoCo analyzes the answers and adapts the future question selection to the reader's cognitive and language level.

Why does CoCoRead help reading?

Conversational reading is a research proven method of effective learning. It promotes critical thinking and language skills of young children. It also leads to higher engagement and better comprehension.
